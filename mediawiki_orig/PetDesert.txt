==Description==
This trigger fires when an NPC leaves its owner for one of the following reasons:
* The owner harmed the NPC.
* The NPC became too hungry.
* The owner hasn't paid the NPC's wages.


Fires on:
* [[Characters#NPCs|NPCs]]
