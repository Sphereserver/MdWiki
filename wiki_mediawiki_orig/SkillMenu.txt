==Description==
This trigger fires when a [[SKILLMENU|skill menu]] is displayed to a player. Note that the trigger only fires when Sphere attempts to display the menu through hardcoded behaviour, using the [[SKILLMENU]] command will not cause this trigger to fire.


Fires on:
* [[Characters#Players|Players]]

| '''Argument''' || '''In/Out''' || '''Description'''
|-
| ARGS || I || The skillmenu's defname. This is expected to be one of the following:
