# Magic Flags

This page lists various flags related to magic effects and their behavior.

-   **`MAGICF_NOREFLECTOWN`**: Blocks reflection of a player's own damage that has already been reflected from another character.
-   **`MAGICF_DELREFLECTOWN`**: Blocks reflection of a player's own damage that has already been reflected from another character (deletion).
