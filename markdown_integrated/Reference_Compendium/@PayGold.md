## @PayGold

This trigger is fired when a character receives a payment. It is used in conjunction with the [PayGold](PayGold) function.

### Arguments

*   **ARGN1**: The amount of gold to pay.
*   **ARGN2**: The type of payment.
    *   0 = Train
    *   1 = Buy
    *   2 = Hire
*   **SRC**: The NPC receiving the money.
*   **ARGO**: The stack of gold used to pay (if any).
