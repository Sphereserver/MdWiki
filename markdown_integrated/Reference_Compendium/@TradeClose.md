## Description

This trigger fires when one player is closing a trade with another player. The trigger will be fired on both characters.

Fires on:

- [Players](Characters#Players "wikilink")

## References

The following object references are explicitly available for this trigger:

| [ARGO](ARGO "wikilink") | The other [player](Characters#Players "wikilink"). |
