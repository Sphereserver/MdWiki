## Crafting Recipes

Sphere supports a variety of crafting recipes. These are the available recipe types:

*   RecipeAlchemy
*   RecipleBlacksmith
*   RecipeBowcraft
*   RecipeCarpentry
*   RecipeCartography
*   RecipeCooking
*   RecipeInscription
*   RecipeTailoring
*   RecipeTinkering
*   RecipeMasonry
*   RecipeGlassblowing
