## PayGold

This function is used to control payment values. It has its own trigger, [@PayGold](@PayGold).

### Arguments

*   **amount**: The amount of gold to pay.
*   **payment_type**: The type of payment.
    *   0 = Train
    *   1 = Buy
    *   2 = Hire
*   **argo**: The stack of gold used to pay (e.g. for training).
