## Properties and Functions {#properties_and_functions}

Here is a list of all attacker properties and functions. If a function
is marked as readable then it can return a value when used as
`<KEY>`{=html}. Click on the name for more detailed information such as
usage and examples.

This parameters must use with
[ATTACKER](#Properties_and_Functions "wikilink"). For examples, please
go [Examples](#Examples "wikilink").

  ----------------------------------- ---------------- --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  **ATTACKER***.key*                  **Read/Write**   **Description**
                                      R                Gets the number of opponents who have damaged the character.
  *n*                                 R                Gets the UID of the nth opponent who has damaged the character. (zero-based)
  [CLEAR](CLEAR "wikilink")           R                Clears the attacker list
  [LAST](LAST "wikilink")             R                Gets the UID of the opponent who most recently damaged the character.
  [MAX](MAX "wikilink")               R                Gets the UID of the opponent who has dealt the most damage to the character.
  [ID](ID "wikilink")                 R                Gets the Attacker.number of the specified character, -1 if its not in the attacker list for this character.
  [TARGET](TARGET "wikilink")         R                Returns the UID of this character\'s best target with this priorities (Threat -\> Distance -\> LOS).
  [ADD](ADD "wikilink")               W                Add this uid to the attacker list.
  [DELETE](DELETE "wikilink")         W                Removes this uid from the attacker list
  *n*.[DAMAGE](DAMAGE "wikilink")     RW               Gets or Sets the amount of damage that the nth opponent has dealt to the character. (zero-based)
  *n*.[ELAPSED](ELAPSED "wikilink")   RW               Gets or Sets the length of time since the nth opponent has damaged the character, in seconds. (zero-based)
  *n*.[UID](UID "wikilink")           R                Gets the UID of the nth opponent who has damaged the character. (zero-based)
  *n*.[THREAT](THREAT "wikilink")     RW               Gets or Sets the Threat generated by the enemy to this character, NPCs will attack the character with most Threat in certain Distance when in LOS (Check for [TARGET](TARGET "wikilink")).
  *n*.[DELETE](DELETE "wikilink")     W                Remove this opponet from the list.
  ----------------------------------- ---------------- --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Examples

The following examples are about
[attacker](#Properties_and_Functions "wikilink").

**ATTACKER**

------------------------------------------------------------------------

`<spherescript>`{=html} \[Events e_example\] On=@Death SERV.LOG Number
Of Attacker: `<attacker>`{=html} `</spherescript>`{=html}

You will see *number of attacker* output to the console.

**ATTACKER***\[.ID\]*\'

------------------------------------------------------------------------

`<spherescript>`{=html} \[EVENTS e_example\] ON=@GetHit Serv.Log
\<src.name\> is the attacker number \'\<attacker.id `<src>`{=html}\>\'
on my list. `</spherescript>`{=html}

**ATTACKER***.n***\[.UID\]**

------------------------------------------------------------------------

`<spherescript>`{=html} \[Events e_example\] On=@Death SERV.LOG First
Attacker: \<uid.\<attacker.0\>.name\> SERV.LOG Fourth Attacker:
\<uid.\<attacker.3.uid\>.name\> `</spherescript>`{=html}

You will see *first and fourth attackers* output to the concole.

OR

`<spherescript>`{=html} \[Events e_example\] On=@Death for 0 \<eval
`<ATTACKER>`{=html}-1\> SERV.LOG \<eval \<DLOCAL.\_FOR\>+1\>. Attacker:
\<uid.\<attacker.\<dlocal.\_for\>\>.name\> endfor
`</spherescript>`{=html}

You will see *all attackers* output to the console.

**ATTACKER.LAST**

------------------------------------------------------------------------

`<spherescript>`{=html} \[Events e_example\] On=@Death SERV.LOG Last
Attacker: \<uid.\<attacker.last\>.name\> `</spherescript>`{=html}

You will see *last attacker* output to the console.

**ATTACKER.MAX**

------------------------------------------------------------------------

`<spherescript>`{=html} \[Events e_example\] On=@Death SERV.LOG Max
Attacker: \<uid.\<attacker.max\>.name\> `</spherescript>`{=html}

You will see *the attacker who dealt the most damage to the character*
output to the console.

**ATTACKER.TARGET**

------------------------------------------------------------------------

`<spherescript>`{=html} \[Events e_example\] On=@Death
ref1=\<attacker.target\> SERV.LOG My current target is: \<ref1.name\>
`</spherescript>`{=html}

This will output in the console the name of the current best target.

Note: Enabling NPC_AI_THREAT in the ini\'s npc_ai settings will make
characters to check for Threat when looking for targets.

**ATTACKER***.n***.DAMAGE**

------------------------------------------------------------------------

`<spherescript>`{=html} \[Events e_example\] On=@Death SERV.LOG Damage
from first player: \<attacker.0.dam\> `</spherescript>`{=html}

You will see *amount of damage which first attacker has dealt* output to
the console.

**ATTACKER***.n***.ELAPSED**

------------------------------------------------------------------------

`<spherescript>`{=html} \[Events e_example\] On=@Death SERV.LOG Attack
time of the first player: \<attacker.0.elapsed\>
`</spherescript>`{=html}

You will see *the length of time since the first opponent has damaged*
output to the console.
