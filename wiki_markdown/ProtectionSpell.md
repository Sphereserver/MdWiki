# Protection Spell

The Protection spell increases the character's resistance to damage.

## Behavior with COMBAT_ELEMENTAL_ENGINE

The behavior of the Protection spell changes depending on the value of the `COMBAT_ELEMENTAL_ENGINE` flag in `sphere.ini`.

When `COMBAT_ELEMENTAL_ENGINE` is enabled, the spell will increase the character's elemental resistances.

When `COMBAT_ELEMENTAL_ENGINE` is disabled, the spell will increase the character's armor class (AR).
